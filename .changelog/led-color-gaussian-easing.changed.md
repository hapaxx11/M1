**LED color easing: Gaussian drop-off** — replaced linear interpolation with a normalized Gaussian curve (`100×(e^(-d²)−e^(-1))/(1−e^(-1))`, where d = depletion fraction). The LED color now stays close to the user-selected color across most of the battery range, then drops off toward the low-battery color with Gaussian curvature as charge depletes. Boundary conditions are preserved: empty battery → low-battery color, full battery → user color.
