**ESP32 capability detection: AT-Custom-Status secondary probe** — `m1_esp32_caps_init()` now tries `AT+GETSTATUSHEX` as a secondary probe when binary `CMD_GET_STATUS` fails. AT firmware variants that implement the `feat/cmd_get_status` extension (`AT+GETSTATUSHEX` returning a hex-encoded 41-byte capability payload) will self-report their capabilities rather than triggering the static fallback profile. Added `m1_esp32_caps_parse_at_hex()` pure-logic helper (header-inline, host-testable) and 8 new unit tests covering valid responses, prefix detection, lowercase hex, truncated/invalid input, and buffer-size guards.
